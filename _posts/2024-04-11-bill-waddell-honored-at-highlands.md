Bill Waddell, chairman of the Judge Waddell Endowed Scholarship Fund and son of Judge C.W. Waddell, was honored at Highlands High School as a special alumnus.

The recognition reflects the deep and ongoing connection between the Waddell family and the Highlands community — a bond that stretches back to the decades Judge Waddell spent building the school's athletic and academic tradition as head football coach and Superintendent of Fort Thomas Schools.
