Highlands High School produced a video capturing highlights from the 2016 Athletic Hall of Fame induction ceremony honoring Coach Ewell "Judge" Waddell.

The video includes background on the Judge's remarkable career at Highlands — as head football coach and later Superintendent of Fort Thomas Schools — along with images from the ceremony itself.
