The Judge Waddell Endowed Scholarship Fund is proud to announce **Ellen Mohr** of Highlands High School as the 2025 Waddell Scholar.

Ellie was selected by the Advisory Committee from a competitive field of applicants, recognized for exemplifying the three pillars at the heart of the scholarship: athleticism, character, and intellect — and for her clear intention to pursue a career in education.

## What Her Nominators Said

Those who put Ellie forward were emphatic:

> "She embodies what every coach wants in a player — her spirit, attitude, and leadership abilities made her a standout, a great teammate. I can think of no better person to teach or coach. She will have a significant impact and influence on the young lives she encounters in her career."

A second nominator described her as "always positive, excited, and optimistic — an ambitious self-starter with a wonderful sense of humor that perfectly balances her intellect, humility, and strong will to achieve. Her dynamic and charismatic personality is contagious. She loves to learn, loves to share, loves to support others, and never loses herself along the way."

## Congratulations, Ellie

The Advisory Committee and the entire Waddell community offer our warmest congratulations to Ellie and her family. We are proud to have her carry forward Judge Waddell's legacy.
