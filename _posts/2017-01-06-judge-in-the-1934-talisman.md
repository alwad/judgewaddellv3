A wonderful piece of Judge Waddell's early history has come to light, courtesy of April McCauley at Western Kentucky University.

Ewell Waddell appears several times in the 1934 Talisman, WKU's yearbook. As a senior, he served as class treasurer, played football, managed basketball, contributed to the Talisman staff, and participated in two clubs. His main entry appears on page 25 of the yearbook — and he was voted most popular boy on campus.

Additional entries appear on pages 27, 88, 99, 106, 122, and 126.

The full 1934 Talisman is available digitally through WKU's TopScholar archive. You can browse it — and search for Ewell Waddell by name — at the link below. Additional years' Talismans are also available in the same collection.
