In 2016, Coach Ewell "Judge" Waddell was inducted into the Highlands High School Athletic Hall of Fame — a recognition long overdue for a man who devoted decades of his life to building the character and competitive spirit of Highlands athletics.

The induction ceremony celebrated the Judge's legacy as head football coach and Superintendent of Fort Thomas Schools, and his lasting influence on the generations of student-athletes who passed through his program from 1940 until his untimely death in 1967.
