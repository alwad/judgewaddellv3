The Judge Waddell Endowed Scholarship Fund is pleased to announce that applications for the **2026 scholarship** are now open.

Highlands High School students who meet the eligibility criteria are encouraged to apply. The scholarship is awarded annually to a student-athlete who demonstrates athleticism, character, and intellect, and who intends to pursue a career in education — as a teacher, coach, or mentor.

## Key Details

- **Eligible applicants:** Highlands High School students
- **Deadline:** March 13, 2026
- **Selection:** Advisory Committee of people who know and love the Highlands tradition

## How to Apply

Download the application, complete all sections, and submit before the deadline.
