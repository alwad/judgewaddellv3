In Memoriam

A Letter from Dr. Homer Rice

Dr. Homer Rice, 1927 – June 10, 2024

About Dr. Rice: Homer Rice was coached at Highlands High School by Judge Waddell, and was later hired by Judge to succeed him as head football coach. He went on to coach at the high school, college, and professional levels, and served as Athletic Director at Georgia Tech — where buildings bear his name and a statue stands outside the stadium alongside John Heisman and Bobby Dodd. He was the principal founder of this scholarship and the author of the Total Person concept. Dr. Rice passed away on June 10, 2024.

The full text of Dr. Rice's letter is available as a PDF. It speaks to the character of Judge Waddell, the meaning of the scholarship, and the values that connect every scholar recipient to the Highlands tradition.
