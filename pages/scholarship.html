The Waddell Scholarship

Awarded annually to a Highlands High School student-athlete who demonstrates the values Judge Waddell spent a lifetime modeling.

About the Scholarship

The Judge Waddell Endowed Scholarship Fund awards an annual scholarship to a graduating Highlands High School student-athlete who embodies the three pillars Judge Waddell built his career around: athleticism, character, and intellect.

Scholarship recipients must intend to pursue a career in education — as a teacher, coach, or mentor — carrying forward the very work that defined Judge Waddell's life.

Eligibility

  • Current student at Highlands High School in Fort Thomas, Kentucky
  • Participated in team athletics during their high school career
  • Demonstrated strong academic performance
  • Intends to pursue a career in teaching, coaching, or education
  • Exhibits the character, leadership, and community spirit the scholarship honors

Selection Criteria

Recipients are chosen by an Advisory Committee made up of people who know and love the Highlands tradition. They are looking for the complete student-athlete — someone who:

  • Leads by example on and off the field
  • Earns the respect of teammates, teachers, and coaches
  • Pursues academic excellence with the same commitment as athletic excellence
  • Demonstrates integrity, humility, and genuine care for others
  • Has a clear intent to give back through a career in education

How to Apply

Download the current application, complete all sections, and submit before the deadline. Applications are reviewed by the Advisory Committee, and recipients are notified in the spring.
