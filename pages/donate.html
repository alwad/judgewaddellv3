Why Your Gift Matters

The Judge Waddell Endowed Scholarship Fund is built on a permanent endowment — meaning your contribution grows over time and supports scholars year after year. Scholarship awards are drawn from the fund's earnings, so principal contributions have lasting, compounding impact.

Every dollar you give helps us:

  • Honor a great man and perpetuate the intellectual and athletic values he stood for
  • Help young people from Highlands High School improve and complete themselves
  • Supply the teachers, coaches, and mentors that future generations will need

As Homer Rice wrote: "That's a great investment."

How to Give

The endowment is managed by The Northern Kentucky Fund of the Greater Cincinnati Foundation, a respected community foundation. Donations are made through their secure platform and are tax-deductible.

About the Endowment

The endowment is permanently held and professionally managed by The Northern Kentucky Fund of the Greater Cincinnati Foundation.

Scholarship awards are made annually from fund earnings, ensuring the Judge's legacy continues indefinitely.
