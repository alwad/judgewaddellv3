Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

The Judge Waddell Endowed Scholarship Fund ("we," "us," or "our") operates judgewaddell.org. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our site.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us — such as your name and email address when you use our contact form. We do not collect payment information directly; donations are processed through the Greater Cincinnati Foundation's secure platform.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect solely to respond to your inquiries and to administer the scholarship program. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

Cookies

This site does not use tracking cookies or analytics beyond what GitHub Pages may collect as part of standard web hosting.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us.
