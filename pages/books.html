Book Orders

Books written by or associated with Homer Rice and the Judge Waddell legacy.

Homer Rice — who was coached and mentored by Judge Waddell at Highlands High School — went on to author several books, including the foundational works behind his Total Person philosophy. This program is active in dozens of college and university athletic departments across the country.

To inquire about book orders, please contact the Fund Administrator or Vice Chairman directly.
