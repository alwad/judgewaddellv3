## High School

Highlands High School, Class of 2007

Sydney starred in soccer at Highlands as goalkeeper, compiling an incredible record with a goals against average of 0.20 her senior year. She was named Gatorade Kentucky Player of the Year and Cincinnati Enquirer Player of the Year, among other honors.

## College

University of Kentucky — compiled one of the best shutout records in UK history. Graduated 2011 with a degree in Elementary Education.

## After Graduation

Sydney served as a substitute teacher in the Ft. Thomas school system and coached at the prestigious Kings Soccer Academy in Wilder, Kentucky, also earning an International Fitness Association (IFA) certification. She is currently a Project Execution Manager for Schneider Electric, managing fifty Project Managers across the East and West coasts — doing a good deal of "teaching" with them regularly. Sydney is also a volunteer youth soccer goalkeeper coach.

On her Highlands experience, Sydney said: "I think Highlands more than prepared me for my college career both as an athlete and also as a student. It is hard for me to believe that I would be where I am now without the help from the wonderful people at Highlands and especially my soccer coach Nina Kearns."
