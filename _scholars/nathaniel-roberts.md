## High School

Highlands High School, Class of 2019

At various points in his Highlands career, Nathaniel was Mr. Highlands Football, selected as Offensive Dominator, and given the Triple Threat Award. He lettered in three different sports and maintained a cumulative 3.7 GPA. Throughout his years at Highlands he took part in and led both academic and athletic programs in Special Education.

His nominators described him as someone who "has a natural talent to connect with kids — brought energy, enthusiasm, and a competitive fire with him every day."

## College

University of Kentucky — graduated May 2023 with a degree in Special Education.

## After Graduation

Nate is a Special Education teacher at Highlands Middle School and a football coach at Highlands High School. He previously served as an Intervention Specialist in the SAIL Department at McNick High School in Cincinnati.
