## High School

Highlands High School, Class of 2022

Abe was a Super Honor Roll student and a four year baseball and basketball player. His basketball team won the State Championship in 2021. Abe graduated Cum Laude, was an AP Scholar, earned straight A's for several semesters, and had perfect attendance for all four years. He was voted Prom King his junior year and to the Homecoming Court his senior year. He participated in National Honors Society, National English Honors Society, National Math Honors Society, Freshman Mentor Council, HHS Organ Donation Club, HHS Investment Club, St. Catherine of Siena Religious Education Program, and Fellowship of Christian Athletes.

Abe also has extensive volunteer experience including HMS Assistant Basketball Coach, Summer Baseball & Basketball Instructor, Honor Flight of Greater Cincinnati, tutoring, HHS Hoxworth Blood Drive, and St. Bernard Food Pantry. He worked as a ball boy for the Cincinnati Reds during summer 2022.

His nominators described him as "a truly special young man — a fantastic student who shows an incredible desire to learn. The type of student who can change the dynamic of a school. He speaks to everyone when he enters a room and thanks his teacher as he leaves. He has an uplifting effect on the morale inside our classrooms."

A second nominator wrote that Abe "genuinely cares about those around him. His effort and energy were infectious. He completes his work with excellence — a young man of the utmost integrity, character, and humility."

## College

Northern Kentucky University — majoring in Secondary Education, Social Studies. Abe is also an executive board member and head of recruitment for the Alpha Tau Omega fraternity.
