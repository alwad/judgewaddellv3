## High School

Highlands High School, Class of 2014

Mallory was captain of the Highlands varsity volleyball team as a senior, after several standout years in that sport, including being named MVP of her freshman team. Mallory was also a member of the tennis and track teams during her Highlands years. Excellent academic achievement and numerous volunteer commitments rounded out a career that led the Scholarship Committee to name her the 2014 recipient.

## College

University of Louisville — graduated 2018 with a degree in Education. During school she continued her volunteer work with everything from Special Olympics to Habitat for Humanity, and was elected VP of the U of L College of Education for 2016–2017.

## After Graduation

Mallory is a 10th Grade Teacher at Walton-Verona High School. She completed her Masters in Educational Administration from Xavier University in 2020.
