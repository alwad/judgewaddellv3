## High School

Highlands High School, Class of 2023

Alyssa was a National Honor Society member who excelled both on the field and in the classroom. She was a four year basketball player who also lettered in soccer, cross country, and track & field. She was Captain of her basketball team and won numerous honors including Most Improved, Triple Threat Award, NKY Co-Miss Hustle, Varsity Miss Hustle, Varsity Defensive Stopper, and 36th District All Tournament Team. Alyssa also made a huge impact in soccer (36th District All Tournament Team), cross country (Newcomer Award, Most Valuable Runner, 110% Award, All State Team ×2, All Region Second Team), and track & field (Newcomer Award, Distance Runner of the Year).

On the academic side Alyssa had a 4.6 GPA while earning straight A's all four years, and was also a member of the National English Honor Society and National Math Honor Society. She was a Title IX Committee member, Babe Ruth Award winner, and participated in Spanish Club. Alyssa has volunteered in the community with Care Closet, the Honor Flight program, and basketball summer camps.

Her nominators described her as "a leader, compassionate, positive mindset, inclusive, role model, hard worker, devoted player, always willing to help." She "pushes her teammates to be the best, but also knows the right words of encouragement — will be an outstanding teacher and coach one day. Her future students and players will love her."

Another nominator wrote that Alyssa "is one of the most admirable students I've ever encountered — she is the epitome of a young lady and a role model. She consistently goes above and beyond. Her compassion and empathy are visible in the community."

## College

University of South Florida — majoring in Elementary Education. Alyssa works in the Tutor-A-Bull program, where USF College of Education students go into local classrooms to tutor students one-on-one or in small groups.
