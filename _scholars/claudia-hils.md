## High School

Highlands High School, Class of 2017

Claudia was an outstanding student-athlete at Highlands, with a concentration in soccer. She was captain of a nationally-ranked soccer team, lettered three times, and coached an elementary soccer team — all while achieving a straight-A academic record and serving in numerous leadership positions, including the National Honor Society.

Her nominators described her as "enthusiastic, optimistic, and joyful — the kind of student who can light up a room, with a strong desire to make the world a better place," "an excellent example of hard work, determination, perseverance, and pride," and noted that "you would be hard pressed to find another young woman who treats her peers and teachers with as much respect and kindness day in and day out while still being fiercely competitive on the soccer field and in the classroom."

## College

Northern Kentucky University — graduated December 2020, Summa Cum Laude with a 4.0 GPA, majoring in Elementary Education. While at NKU she held leadership positions in the Student Government Association, NKU Women's Club Soccer Team, Delta Zeta sorority, and Norse Leadership Society. She served as an Orientation Leader to incoming freshmen, a peer mentor to college students with learning disabilities, and was the only student representative on the College of Education Dean Search Committee in 2019–2020. She completed her student teaching at Ruth Moyer Elementary in Fort Thomas and was honored with the Outstanding Teacher Candidate Award for Elementary Education for Fall 2020.

## After Graduation

Claudia is a 5th Grade Teacher at Ruth Moyer Elementary in Ft. Thomas. She completed her Masters of Arts in Education: Teacher as Leader (Curriculum and Instruction) from NKU in May 2023 and her Rank I Degree in Gifted and Talented Education from NKU in May 2024.
