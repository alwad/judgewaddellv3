## High School

Highlands High School, Class of 2016

McKenzie was a four-year Super Honor Roll student-athlete at Highlands. She received the Presidential Award for Academic Excellence and the distinction of being a 2015 Kentucky Governor's Scholar. She was a member of National Honor Society, National English Honor Society, National Spanish Honor Society, and Future Business Leaders of America.

McKenzie was a three-year starter on the Varsity Girls Basketball team, earning 5 Varsity letters and serving as team captain her senior year. She was named MVP of the LaRosas Holiday Classic Tournament in 2015 and to the 9th Region All-Tournament team in 2014, 2015, and 2016. In 2015 she received the Highlands High School David Cecil Citizenship Award by unanimous vote from all Varsity athletic coaches. She also received the Northern Kentucky High School Girls Basketball Coaches' Association Division II Academic Award.

Her nominators described her as "the ultimate team player — has the patience and work ethic that will make her a great teacher in the classroom" and as someone who "treats all of her peers with the utmost respect and kindness."

## College

University of Kentucky — graduated 2020 with a degree in Elementary Education and a minor in Spanish. She also received the Presidential Scholarship covering full tuition for four years.

## After Graduation

McKenzie is a 5th Grade Teacher at Ruth Moyer Elementary in Ft. Thomas and an Assistant Coach for Girls Varsity Basketball at Highlands High School. She previously taught 4th Grade at Grandview Elementary and was a Varsity Assistant Coach for the Bellevue High School Girls Basketball Team.
