## High School

Highlands High School, Class of 2019

Will personifies the student-athlete profile the Scholarship seeks to support. Twice a football Captain, and recipient of the Red Dog Spirit Award his junior year, Will's teachers, coaches, and colleagues all speak of his determination, dedication, and grit in whatever he takes on.

His nominators described him as someone who "truly embodies our team core value of adding value to the lives of those around you — a completely selfless individual who always puts others first."

## College

Northern Kentucky University — graduated December 2024 with a degree in Education. During his time at NKU he participated in the COST study abroad program in Cologne, Germany for Fall 2024.

## After Graduation

Will is currently a 6th Grade Intervention Specialist at Ockerman Middle School.
