## High School

Highlands High School, Class of 2020

Brycen was a middle linebacker and Team Captain of the football team. Among his numerous awards he was named team MVP, 1st Team All-State, 1st Team All-Tristate, Northern KY Defensive Player of the Year, and All-Decade Team. In the classroom Brycen excelled as a Super Honor Roll student with a 3.9 GPA. He also won the Leadership Award, volunteered for the Spring Clean event, served as an Assistant Jr. Football Coach, and was a Moyer Elementary Hero for 2nd grade students.

His nominators described him as having "the highest level of character and integrity that we all want represented in the teaching profession" and as "an advocate for all students — kind to everyone on a daily basis."

## College

Eastern Michigan University (2020–2022), Georgetown College (2023– )

Brycen plays varsity football and is majoring in Elementary Education. At EMU he was named Special Teams Scout Team Player of the Year and earned Scout Team Player of the Week honors twice during the 2020 season.
