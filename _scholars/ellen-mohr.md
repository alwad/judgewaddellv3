## High School

Highlands High School, Class of 2025

Ellie was a National Honor Society member who made a big impact both on the court and in the classroom. She was a four year varsity letter winner in Volleyball, and two year varsity letter winner in Basketball and Track & Field. She won the Highlands Volleyball Ace Award her senior year, and the Student-Athlete of the Year award her Freshman year. Ellie served as Class Vice President, Title IX Female Representative, and Green Dot Representative. She was in the Leadership Club and a Student Section (Ozone) Leader, and was nominated to both Prom Court and Homecoming Court. She was awarded the Community Service Merit Award her senior year. Ellie has helped coach a youth basketball team, as well as coach, ref, and scorekeep for the 'Lil Birds' volleyball program.

Her nominators described her as someone who "embodies what every coach wants in a player — her spirit, attitude, and leadership abilities made her a standout, a great teammate. I can think of no better person to teach or coach. She will have a significant impact and influence on the young lives she encounters in her career."

Ellie is "always positive, excited, and optimistic — an ambitious self-starter with a wonderful sense of humor that perfectly balances her intellect, humility, and strong will to achieve. Her dynamic and charismatic personality is contagious. She loves to learn, loves to share, loves to support others, and never loses herself along the way."
