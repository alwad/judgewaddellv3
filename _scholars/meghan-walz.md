## High School

Highlands High School, Class of 2020

Meghan was an Honor Roll student and four year member of the Basketball program. She excelled in the classroom as a National Honor Society student with a 3.9 GPA. Meghan was heavily involved in her community and volunteered at the Take Flight Basketball League, Highlands Youth Basketball Camps, Luke Muller Golf Outing, and Highlands Athletics Booster Drive.

Her nominators described her as "kind, ethical, diligent, and well-grounded — not only is she a leader on the court and in the locker room, she is a leader amongst the student body" and "a well-rounded, caring, and responsible young woman. What makes Meghan truly stand out from her peers is her character and drive to better the lives of those around her."

## College

Northern Kentucky University (2020–2023), University of Arkansas (2024– )

Meghan graduated from NKU in December 2023 with a degree in Marketing. During her time at NKU she was the Director of Basketball Operations for the Northern Kentucky Women's Basketball team. Beginning Fall 2024, Meghan attends the University of Arkansas as a Graduate Assistant in the Athletic Business Office while pursuing a Master's degree.
