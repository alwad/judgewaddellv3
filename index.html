The Judge

Coach. Mentor. Legend.

Judge C.W. Waddell was a towering figure in Northern Kentucky athletics and education — a man whose influence on his players extended far beyond the field. He produced coaches who went on to shape generations of student-athletes, including Homer Rice, who built on the Judge's "Total Person" philosophy into a nationally recognized program at Georgia Tech.

The scholarship bears his name as a tribute to his belief that athletics, academics, and character are inseparable — and that the greatest outcome of sport is the creation of better teachers, coaches, and citizens.
